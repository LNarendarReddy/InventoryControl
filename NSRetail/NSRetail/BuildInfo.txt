		o	Once all items are entered, the user must click "Initiate Credit Note".
		o	The sheet will then be saved with the status Credit Note Initiated.
		o	After the supplier return is physically received, the user will update the return status, enter the return value, and generate the credit note.

		o	Supplier Returns will be allowed only when sufficient stock exists under the Damage & Wastage branch.
